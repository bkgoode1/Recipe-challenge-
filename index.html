Simple Omelette Recipe

An easy and quick dish, perfect for any meal. The classic omelellete combines beaten eggs cooked to perfection,optionally filled with your choice of cheese, vegetables, and meats.

preperation time

  • total: approxiamtely 10 minutes
  • prep:5 minutes
  • cooking:5 minutes

Ingredients

  • 2-3 large eggs
  • salt, to taste
  • pepper, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • optional fillings: cheese,diced vegetables,cooked meats, herbs

Instructions

  1. Beat the eggs: in a bowl, beat the eggs with a pinch of salt and pepper until well mixed. you can add a tablespoon of water or milk for a fluffler texture
  2. heat the pan: place a non-stick frying pan over medium heat and add butter or oil
  3. cook the omelette: once the butter is melted, pour in the eggs. tilt the pan to ensure the eggs coat the surface
  4. add fillings: when the eggs begin the set at the edges but are still slighty runny in the middle, sprinkle your chosen fillings over the omelette
  5. fold and serve: as the omelette continues to cook,carefully lift one edge and fold it over the fillings. let it cook another minute, then slide it onto a plate.
  6. enjoy: serve hot, with salt and pepper if needed.
